# Roux Resources

In this website, you will find resources for the Roux method. This resource was intentionally made to be fully accessible and editable by the Roux community. If you have resources that you believe are worth contributing, please do so by looking at the [contributing page](./docs/contributing.md).

A very good Roux method website containing many other websites can be found at [rouxcubing.com](https://rouxcubing.com/). If you would like to join our Discord server where much of our conversation happens, or if you just have a question about what you could do, you can find a [link here](https://discord.com/invite/VJunGCZBGZ).
